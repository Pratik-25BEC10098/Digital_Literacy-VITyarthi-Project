Task 5 - Part B  
Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:  

Do's for responsible social media use:- 

 Enable two-factor authentication on all your social media accounts.  
 Always make a strong password using a mix of letters, numbers and special characters.  
 Verify the information or messages before sharing.  
 Always think before you post or comment.  
 Credit the original artist or creator before using their work.    


Don'ts for responsible social media use:- 

 Never ignore privacy and security settings.  
 Never engage in trolling or cyberbullying.  
 Don't waste excessive time on social media or reel scrolling.  
 Do not click on any suspicious link on mail or text messages.  
 Do not compare your life with other’s lives on social media.
