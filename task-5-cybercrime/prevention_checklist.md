Task 5 - Part B  
Create a 'Stay Safe Online' checklist with at least 8 specific, actionable prevention tips relevant to college students in India:-

Enable two-factor authentication on email, banking apps and all social media accounts.  
Always make a strong password using a mix of letters, numbers and special characters.  
Do not click on any suspicious link on mail or text messages.  
Always check the sender email IDs as scammers often mimic official mail addresses.  
Avoid logging into sensitive accounts on unauthorised Wifi or public Wifi.  
Always install any app from official stores like Google play store or Apple App store and avoid using any APKs.  
Verify college notices through official websites or faculty, not forwarded messages.  
Never share OTP to anyone.  
While receiving the money, never put your PIN.  
In case of fraud, report the case to National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call at helpline number 1930.
